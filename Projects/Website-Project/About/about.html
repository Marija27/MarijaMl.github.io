We are Blond.

studio with an Industrial

Blond is a London based design studio, founded in 2014. We create
contemporary products, spaces and digital experiences for a diverse range of
brands, ranging from boutique start-ups to global businesses

Who We Are

James Melia

Founder & Creative Director

Following a degree in Furniture & Product Design from Nottingham Trent University, James worked for some of the world's leading design agencies. He also spent several years as Design Manager at a London based brand - where he developed a passion for leading creative projects.

During his career James has collaborated with brands such as - Microsoft, Philips, Nokia, Qatar Airways, Virgin Atlantic, LG, Samsung, Coway, Midea, B/E Aerospace, Hasbro and Tomy; on everything from mobile phones to televisions to business-class suites.

Mike Goyen

Partner & Business Director

Mike also studied Furniture & Product Design. Since then Mike has workedfor several major London kitchen and interior design consultancies.During this time he developed key technical skills that led him into project management.


Mike has years of experience leading large commercial projects for a range of clients that include - Berkeley Homes, Mace Group, Allenbuild and Balfour Beatty.

What We Do

Our work spans multiple disciplines, including; Industrial Design, Product
Design, Branding, UX & UI, Packaging and Visualisation.
Our name is inspired by a colour of wood - a subtle, muted and unobtrusive
colour. Our philosophy is hidden within this. We believe that good design
answers questions, is intuitive and brings benefit to the user; without
shouting. We strive for simple solutions to what are often complex problems.
We listen and translate our client’s requirements into straightforward and
effective design strategy. We deliver our clients a thoughtful and refined
service, with passion and free from ego.
