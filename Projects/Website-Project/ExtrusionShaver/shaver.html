Project Information

Project: Extrusion Shaver
Disciplines: Industrial Design / Visualisation
Client: Blond (Conceptual)
Year: 2017

The Extrusion Shaver has been designed in response to a market filled with
over-styled, throw-away products. Our aim was to design a premium electric
shaver with simplified functionality, neutral colour direction and increased
material quality. A shaver designed to last for decades and suitable for all
tastes. Although our process was driven by longevity and quality - by utilising
mass production techniques (extruded aluminum) the shaver has been designed
for low cost production.
