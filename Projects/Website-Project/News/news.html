28th June 2016

New Hide Watch Colours

We are pleased to announce that our Hide Watch has received a fantastic
reaction since its release. Due to this, ahead of its launch later this year - we
have recently designed four new colour variants. We will be posting more
images closer to the launch. Sign up to our newsletter for product updates,
exhibitions and further studio news.

8th April 2016

Studio Celebration

Last evening we finally had the chance to celebrate the renovation of our
Hackney studio. Thank you to everyone who visited and made it a great
evening! We will be opening our doors for the London Design Festival in
September - as part of the Icon Design Trail, please drop-by and say hello.

25th January 2016

Maison et Objet 2016

This year the Blond team attended Maison et Objet. We visited this renowned
Paris design fair with our friends at Yod & Co - London based lifestyle brand,
who's website will be launching in early 2016. We spent two days soaking up
the the sights of Paris and new designs from an extensive list of exhibitors.
Maison proves to be a great show year-on-year, with plenty to see;
from electronics to fashion to homewares - we recommend you visit!

29th October 2015

New Business Cards

As part of the new Blond branding, we are delighted to finally showcase our
business cards. We worked hard to convey our brand ethos with the
overarching branding. We have brought this forward into the business card
design. The stock was carefully chosen from the range by
G.F.Smith. The print and foil-blocking was the work of Generation Press,
Brighton based print-press. We are very happy with the outcome, thank you all those involved!
