Build a TV stand to suit your personality.

Project Information

Project: Personality TV Stand
Disciplines: Industrial Design
Client: Samsung & Dezeen Competition
Year: 2017

When decorating an interior, you have an endless amount of possibilities. As a
result your environment is unique to you; a reflection of your personality and
style. For the TV to truly to become a lifestyle product, we must first create
choice. With this choice you can tailor your product to your lifestyle. Driven by
this goal, we have created a modular design consisting of three simple parts;
enabling the buyer freedom to configure their TV stand in a way which
reflects their personality.
