The Fenn Chair is a modern interpretation of classic archetypes and processes.

Project Information

Project: Fenn Chair
Disciplines:Industrial Design / Visualisation
Client: Blond
Year: 2015

The Fenn Chair is an honest design that is sensitive to modern-day use and
manufacturing. This minimal design utilises the processes of molded
plywood lamination and steam-bending, with no visible joints and fittings.
The chair has a scallop on the underside of the steam-bent oak legs to allow
the chairs to nestle together securely whilst stacked.
